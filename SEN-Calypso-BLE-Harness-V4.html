Activation handshake

Proven sequence (FFF1, \r\r terminated): :DU,1668647648,A7 · :GK · :PS,1,1,9F · :WU,5 · :WU,6 · :VR, then :CC,6,86 every 10s. Try the extra temp/RH probes below if temp/RH don't appear from discovery.

Temp/RH probe commands · experimentation

If discovery doesn't reveal temp/RH, the app may request them with a command we haven't seen. These are educated guesses based on the protocol's verb style (:TH temp/humidity, :EN environment, :WU,7 higher mode). Send one, then watch for new data.
